Project Details

This was a project for school were we had to make a game based on our experiences during a trip to Berlin.

We decided to combine two experiences that really stood out to us.
During our trip we visited the Monsterkabinett which inspired us to make a game with metallic monsters and loud noises.
In Berlin we had some struggles with a ticketmachine at a trainstation, this frustation of operating a slow machine, that you aren't able to fully understand was an experience we wanted to recreate.

Monster Machine is a vr experiece where the player interacts with a ticketmachine to hopefully order a ticket.
At first the interactions are fairly normal, press a button, pull a lever, etc.
But then the machine changes, meat grows from the crevices and the button is replaced with an eyeball.
When the player presses the eye just like the button previously they will feel a wet ball in real life simulating the gross flesh of the machine.
The game keeps escalating and the machine gets more disgusting untill eventually the players has to cut open the monster machine, grab the heart and then the game suddenly cuts back to reality with a normal, but broken ticket machine and a ticket instead of a heart.

The game is operated by a game master who controls the interactions and progress of the game. 2 others bring the physical objects to the player.

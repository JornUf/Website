The classic game of operation, with real consequences!

A classic game...

Dr Furby is a simply a large version of the classic childhood game operation, but with a dark twist...

Real consequences!

Now you have a real patient! That patient will even be shocked if the doctor makes a mistake.

You better listen!

The doctor should listen to the furby or suffer the consequences themselves! But you can trust Furby right?

Project Details

This was our game for project hybrid.

The goal of project hybrid is to make a game that bridges the gap between the real and digital world. However we had anohter goal, to make a project so weird that it would become well known.

Another goal of ours was to play around with breaking "the magic circle" and introducing real consequences to a game.

During this project I worked on some of the safety research, designing the user experience and programming the electronic components.
